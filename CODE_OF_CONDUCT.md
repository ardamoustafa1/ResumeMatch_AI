# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior include empathy, constructive feedback, respect for
different viewpoints and responsibility for mistakes. Harassment, threats,
personal attacks, sexualized attention and publication of private information are
unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior through the repository owner's private contact or
GitHub reporting mechanisms. Maintainers may remove, edit or reject contributions
and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors whose behavior is harmful.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
