# ResumeMatch AI Extension Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 20, 2026

## Information We Collect
The ResumeMatch AI extension does not collect, track, or share any personal information.
- The extension extracts job description text from the active LinkedIn tab.
- This data is sent securely to the ResumeMatch AI backend configured by the user.

## Data Storage
- Your API key and CV text are stored locally in your browser's **session storage**.
- This data is erased when you close your browser, ensuring no sensitive plaintext keys remain on your disk.

## Third-Party Services
The extension communicates exclusively with the backend URL you specify in the settings.

## Contact
If you have any privacy concerns, please contact the maintainer of your ResumeMatch AI deployment.
