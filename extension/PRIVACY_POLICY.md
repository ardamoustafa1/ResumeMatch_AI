# ResumeMatch AI Extension Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 20, 2026

## Information We Process
The ResumeMatch AI extension does not operate an advertising or analytics service.
- The extension extracts job description text from the active LinkedIn tab.
- Your CV text and the extracted job description are sent to the ResumeMatch AI
  backend configured by you when you explicitly start an analysis.

## Data Storage
- Your API key, backend URL, and CV text are stored in Chrome's
  `storage.local` area on your device so they remain available after restart.
- Remove the extension or clear its settings to delete this local data.
- Treat the API key as a password. Revoke it from the ResumeMatch dashboard if
  the device is lost or shared.

## Third-Party Services
The extension communicates exclusively with LinkedIn job pages and the backend
URL you explicitly configure. The configured backend may use an external AI
provider according to that deployment's privacy policy.

## Contact
If you have any privacy concerns, please contact the maintainer of your ResumeMatch AI deployment.
