# Support

Need help with NetworkForge? We have several channels available for support, depending on your needs.

## Community Support
For general questions, troubleshooting, and community discussions:
- **Discord:** [Join the NetworkForge Discord Server](https://discord.gg/networkforge) (Recommended for fast answers)
- **GitHub Discussions:** Use the [Discussions tab](https://github.com/networkforge/discussions) for Q&A and architecture questions.
- **GitHub Issues:** Use Issues strictly for bug reports and feature requests.

## Enterprise & SLA Support
If you are deploying NetworkForge in a corporate environment and require guaranteed response times or custom integration assistance:
- **Email:** Contact us at `enterprise@networkforge.ai`
- **SLA:** We offer tailored Service Level Agreements for production deployments.

## Security Issues
If you have discovered a security vulnerability, **do not open a public issue**. Please refer to `SECURITY.md` for instructions on how to securely disclose it to our team.
